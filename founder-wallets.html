What is a Founder Wallet?

A Founder Wallet makes you a founder in the DYNK ecosystem. Numbered 0001–0800, each wallet is a unique on-chain NFT that permanently records your place in the founding network.

Founder Wallets sit at the top of the DYNK structure. Transaction fees generated across the network are distributed among eligible founders, creating a direct link between network activity and founder rewards.

When you hold a Founder Wallet and maintain a balance of 10,000 DYNK or more, you remain eligible for your share of transaction fees and for rewards across three levels of referrals. You are not just a customer. You are part of the foundation of the ecosystem.
