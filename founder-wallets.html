What is a Founder Wallet?

A DYNK founder wallet unlocks full potential earnings, governance power and a NFT wallet that accumulates value as you interact with the platform.

Each wallet is unique on-chain permanently recording your place in the founding network.

When you hold a Founder Wallet and maintain a balance of 10,000 DYNK or more, you remain eligible for an equal share in transaction fees in the network, 3 levels of referrals, and governance in decisions on the DYNK network.

Through our in-app marketplace you can sell the NFT wallet effectively monetising the wallet allowing it to function as a transferable asset.

Founder wallets also receive a physical cold wallet card powered by Tangem's industry leading hardware security.
